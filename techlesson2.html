Remember that customer service is key.

One of the biggest parts of the job disription is the ability to work well with customers. Anybody associated with the service desk should be committed to providing good, if not outstanding, customer service. This is one of the more important parts of the job that will directly affect your interactions with your customers.

Answer the phone properly

Another big part of The help desk role is how well you answer the phone. This directly ties into how good your customer service is. If your company has a particular script for answering phones you should use that, but often time a simple "Hello, My name is Corey, how may I help you?" will suffice. Remember to alsways be courteous when conducting bussiness over the phone. Your disposition has a big effect on the customer that is calling in, and you being polite and helpfull will ease them into a better mood.
